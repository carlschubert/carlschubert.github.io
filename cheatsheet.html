CSS Cheat Sheet

Margin, Padding, Boarder

  • margin is the space outside the border
  • margin 5px ------ top, bottom, left, and right and one property
  • margin 5px 10px ------- top and bottom, left and right
  • margin 5px 10px 15px ------ top and bottom, left, right
  • margin 5px 10px 15px 25px ------ top, right, bottom, left seperatly
  • padding is the space between border and content
  • padding 5px ------ top, bottom, left, and right and one property
  • padding 5px 10px ------- top and bottom, left and right
  • padding 5px 10px 15px ------ top and bottom, left, right
  • padding 5px 10px 15px 25px ------ top, right, bottom, left seperatly
  • border has 3 attributes
  • border 2px solid black
  • solid, dashed, inset, outset, dotted, hidden, double
